Functionality

1. Automatic pulling/pushing of sales orders to third-party ERP/WMS/CRM/BI systems.

2. Easy cloud and SaaS-based deployment.

3. No customization required.

4. Supports simplified Chinese, English, etc.

5. Easy to configure.

6. Monitors application performance.

7. Advanced data protection policy.

8. Multi-currency: Hold local currency accounts in 7 major currencies - USD, CAD, GBP, HKD, EUR, AUD and SGD.

9. Make payments: Pay taxes and suppliers in local currency and avoid unnecessary conversion fees.

10. Get paid: Receive funds from Amazon, payment gateways and directly from your customers.

11. Free upgrade to annual package.

12. Free upgrades for enhanced ERP/WMS/CRM/BI system support.

Description

Helps sellers improve listing information, inventories, orders and other operational capabilities through programmatic means. Provides brands with decision support through data analytics.

Who should use this?

SellerMatrix is the perfect tool for you if you:

1. Sell FBA or merchant-fulfilled items on Amazon

2. Fulfills FBM and FBA orders by obtaining Amazon orders

3. Automatic shipments via API

4. Sell on platforms other than Amazon

5. Automatic downloads of inventory snapshots

6. Automated listing post information

7. Sell a large number of SKUs with new SKUs being added all the time

8. Need a way to quickly analyze inventory lots to make buying decisions

9. Need a way to quickly list and ship a large number of items
